# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to Sovereign Founder OS — regardless of background, experience level, gender identity, nationality, or area of expertise.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them find their footing
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people
- Accept responsibility and apologize when mistakes are made
- Prioritize the safety and privacy of users in design discussions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sharing security vulnerabilities publicly before coordinated disclosure
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional open source community

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate.

Report conduct concerns by opening a private issue with maintainers or via the contact method listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
